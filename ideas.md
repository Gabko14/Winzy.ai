# Ideas

## Flame Forecast

A forward-looking layer for The Flame that shows how near-future actions affect
consistency instead of only reflecting past behavior.

### Why it fits Winzy.ai

- Strengthens the habit-tracker-first core before adding more social complexity
- Makes the consistency-over-streaks philosophy tangible and emotionally clear
- Reuses existing flame, consistency, and challenge concepts instead of
  introducing a disconnected system
- Can later enrich Today, Habit Detail, the public flame page, and challenge
  surfaces

### Core experience

Tell the user the smallest next action that meaningfully protects or grows their
flame.

Examples:

- "Log today and your flame stays strong."
- "Skip today and nothing collapses, but you'll need 2 check-ins by Sunday to
  recover."
- "Hit 3 of your next 5 scheduled days and you'll reach 78%."

### Product notes

- This should make Winzy feel like a windshield, not just a rearview mirror.
- It must stay supportive and never feel punishing or guilt-driven.
- Keep this as a saved concept for later, not a commitment to implement now.
